Welcome to The Amazing Evolution of Hip Hop!!!

This site brings a lesson of the history of Hip Hop and has all you need to know about the highlights of each of the decades. In each page you will find a breif history about the specific decade, a detailed description of the history of influtial artist of the decade, and some of the best hits hip hop has to offer. The website's creators, Shaun Mckellar and Nicholas Wilson share a love for hip hop music and have listened to various artists that provide different flows and techniques when putting their lyrics together. Please feel free to leave a comment on the pages if you have suggestions on artists or music to add to the site. Every suggestion is not promised to appear but we will try to accomendate our users to the best of our ability. Also try out our trivia questionare on each page to see what you learned! Thank you and enjoy The Amazing Evolution of Hip Hop!
